Antwerp Scheldelaan (N101) will receive new asphalt pavement

The Roads and Traffic Agency will start on Monday, October 3 with asphalting work on the N101 Scheldelaan at Zandvliet (Antwerp). The present road from the northern land bridge to the Antwerpsebaan is in poor condition and need replacing.

The works to Scheldelaan starting Oct. 3 at 6 am Monday morning until Friday 14 October. l the Agency starts the road construction between Antwerpsebaan and Noordland bridge. The contractor is replacing the worn asphalt pavement. Motorists are also effected, as during the two weeks Scheldelaan between Noordland bridge and Antwerpsebaan will be in either direction completely interrupted. The slip roads from the A12 to N101 Scheldelaan at Zandvliet are closed during the works.

Who wants to be on site, in both directions must detour via the A12, Harbour Centre, R2 and exit Lillo Scheldelaan.
